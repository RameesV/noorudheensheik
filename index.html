Who am i

My name is Noorudheen Sheik, I was born on 25 may1988 in famous family called sheik House wayanad Kerala. I got married at the age of 26 with Shamna and blessed with a baby called Fathima Jaza binth Noorudheen Sheik on 2017. I started to do some social activities for the poor people, after coming back to Kerala I completely concentrated on this field and I started to do my vlogging life to, vlogging and anchoring are my passion. And done many things and still doing. 2020 I started a new program called Better Tomorrow Initiative, in this journey of love mainly doing well donation for the north Indian peoples, who have not tasted pure water in their life. My plan was donate 250 wells in north Indians, also help the childrens in the north area by giving the essentials like food cloths and their study materials. And I am much happy with this work. My dream is to do help for the poor people in the country like Africa and Somalia. I am a travel vlogger and I do promotion works and branding through my Facebook page with millions of viewers.
